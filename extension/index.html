Welcome to the Safe Internet Coalition.

SIC provides an extension to hide sensitive topics as you browse.

It works seamlessly in the background to dynamically remove unwanted topics from pages.

Add keyword

These keywords will be dynamically be removed from pages as you browse. Every keyword you add up to 10 associated words are found.

Catagories

These catagories will be censored as you browse.

Controls

Configure SIC to your liking.

Controls whether filtering is active. Disabling the filter always shows the original content.

Opens SIC's global settings. You can add and remove sensitive topics here.
